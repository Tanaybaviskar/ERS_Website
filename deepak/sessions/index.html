ABOUT US

Welcome to the Electronics and Robotics Society, where passion for technology brings us together. As the oldest club in the institute, we've built a community that thrives on curiosity, creativity, and collaboration. Whether you're just starting out or you've been tinkering for years, you'll find a place here.

We're dedicated to helping everyone learn and grow. Our beginner-friendly sessions make it easy to dive into the world of electronics and robotics, and our competitions, like RoboRush, give you a chance to put your skills to the test in a fun, supportive environment.

At the heart of everything we do is a love for building, experimenting, and turning ideas into reality. Join us, and let's create something amazing together!
